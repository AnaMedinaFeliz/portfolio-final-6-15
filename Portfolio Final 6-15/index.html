Ana Medina Feliz

My passion for design / UX / UI ....

I have been a registered nurse for over 6 years and I am passionate about advocating for my patients and families, and delivering patient centered care. Empathy is a skill that I have gained as a nurse, and it has shaped how I identify and solve problems.

In the wake of new emerging technology, I have always thought about using design to solve and improve medical software programs.   I contemplate about the impact of making more intentional and informed design decisions in medical information platforms, and how design decisions can positively impact not only healthcare, but the world. Hence, why I am now a UX/UI designer.

I specialize in secondary research, Figma, HTML and CSS. I enjoy solving user complex usability issues, focusing on improving information architecture, design, accessibility and user experience. As a researcher, I can transform user research and data into actionable results.
